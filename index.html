About

Tunga Güngör is a professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Boğaziçi University. His research interests include natural language processing, artificial intelligence, and computational linguistics.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
  • Computational Linguistics
  • Text Mining and Information Retrieval

Selected Publications

  1. Güngör, T. et al. Turkish NLP Resources and Applications, Journal of Language Technology, 2024.
  2. Güngör, T., & Co-authors. Deep Learning Approaches for Morphologically Rich Languages, ACL 2023.
  3. Güngör, T., Advances in Turkish Text Processing, Springer, 2022.

Teaching

  • CMPE 58M – Machine Learning for Text
  • Graduate Seminars on Computational Linguistics
